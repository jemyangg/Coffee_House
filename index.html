Our Menu

Explore our menu featuring a variety of handcrafted coffees, premium teas, and delectable pastries. From espresso classics to signature blends, we have something to satisfy every palate.

Cappuccino

A classic espresso-based drink with steamed milk and a layer of froth.

Espresso

Strong and rich black coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans.

Latte

Smooth espresso mixed with steamed milk and topped with a small amount of foam.

Americano

Black coffee made by diluting espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength but different flavor than drip coffee.

Macchiato

Espresso with a small amount of milk, usually foamed, added.

Mocha

Espresso mixed with chocolate syrup and steamed milk, often topped with whipped cream.

Croissant

Light and flaky pastry made with butter and characterized by its crescent shape.

Baguette

Long, thin loaf of French bread known for its crispy crust and soft interior.

Cinnamon Roll

Sweet roll with a cinnamon and sugar filling, typically topped with icing.

About Us

Welcome to CoffeeHouse, where passion meets perfection in every cup. Our journey began with a simple idea - to create a haven for coffee enthusiasts and tea lovers alike.

At CoffeeHouse, we are dedicated to providing an exceptional experience. Our team of skilled baristas carefully selects the finest coffee beans and tea leaves to ensure each sip is a moment of pure delight.

Step into our cozy and inviting atmosphere, where the aroma of freshly brewed beverages mingles with the warmth of friendly conversations. Whether you're here for a quick pick-me-up or to unwind with friends, CoffeeHouse is the place to be.

Join us on a journey of flavors, where every cup tells a story. We invite you to be a part of the CoffeeHouse family and savor the perfect brew crafted just for you.
